Welcome to xTiber

Here you can explore different hypotheses in a rational way using Bayes' theorem. In a certain sense this is like a "vote calculator", where you specify your views on certain facts and you receive a 'verdict'. The whole point is to be aware of the arguments and your assessment of them, and see how they affect your overall belief in the hypothesis. We believe this is the most rational way to approach the most complex questions, where certainty is not possible.
